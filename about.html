About Me

Konnichiwa! My name is Ellie!

So, a little bit about me (on more of a personal note) I am a 33-year-old (female in case you were wondering), currently living in London, originally from Cambridgeshire.

I begin to watch Anime back when I was 15 years old, my first one if I can remember right was Gravitation (not to sure why that one!) which moved onto Gundam Wing, Fullmetal Alchemist and Death Note to name a few starters! From this my interest for Japanese music took off listening to what was known at the time Visual Kei ; the strong leaders being Dir en Grey, D’espairsRay and Kagerou (which I end up seeing in Germany when they toured!)

My Love for Anime re-surfaced back into my life back in 2020 (during the Covid lockdown times!) through the spider web of faithful YouTube videos and stumbling on Anime opening themes, I thought to myself ‘Huh’ why did I stop watching this? And low and behold a year later I am now making a good dent into my never-ending Anime list! This has also brought on some amazing Japanese artists as well. My Work is mainly a celebration of the Anime I have enjoyed over the years and hope I have captured them in a due and worthy light!

Drawing also helps with my creative side, as I love to also knit and crochet as well, but I want to express my newfound enjoyment into drawing!

If you would like to know more then feel free to write to me, or if you have any Anime suggestions I would love to hear from you too! :D

Ja na!

Ellie
